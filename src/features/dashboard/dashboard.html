view3c

Upload your W3C log file by clicking the button below or by dragging a file into it.

Various graphs will be available to visualize your data.

About

Visualize IIS logs in your browser. No download or sign-ups required.

Your files always stay local -- they are never uploaded to a remote server. Go ahead, turn your Wifi off.

Upload and process multiple log files at once. They will automatically be ordered chronologically.

Built with Apache Echarts.

Inspired by simple*, useful websites such as draw.io and trace.playwright.dev

*Simple as in clean UI and easy to use. Both examples are technologically fantastic and complex.

Fully open source.
